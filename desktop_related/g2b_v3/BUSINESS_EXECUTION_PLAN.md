# G2B Business Execution Plan

## 1) Goal
- Build a repeatable monthly revenue engine from `latest_all.json` lead data.
- Target the first paid cohort with fast-cycle discretionary contracts (`재량계약`, `소액수의`).
- Keep operations stable enough to run weekly without manual babysitting.

## 2) Product Packaging
- `Quick Win` (1.2M KRW / case)
  - Small discretionary opportunities, rapid scope, one-shot support.
- `Standard` (1.8M KRW / case)
  - Broader documentation support + bid decision support.
- `Premium` (2.5M KRW / case)
  - Higher budget and/or more complex bid opportunities.
- `Retainer Lite` (390K KRW / month)
  - Weekly lead list + alert digest + shortlist curation.
- `Retainer Pro` (990K KRW / month)
  - Lite + priority lead triage + monthly strategy review.

## 3) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- SME contractors that cannot monitor public opportunities daily.
- Firms with 1-5 active bidding staff and unstable pipeline.
- Early target verticals:
  - Civil and electrical contractors
  - Local-region construction service providers
  - Agencies needing continuous bid funnel support

## 4) Sales Funnel (Operational)
- Input:
  - `result/sales_queue_latest.csv` (generated by `business_ops.py`)
  - `result/sales_accounts_latest.csv`
- Weekly cadence:
  - Top 50 lead outreach
  - 10 discovery calls
  - 4 proposal submissions
  - 2 closes target
- Funnel definitions:
  - Contacted -> Meeting -> Proposal -> Closed

## 5) KPI Targets (First 90 Days)
- Week 1-2:
  - Fill `clients.csv` with at least 30 target client records.
  - Start weekly pipeline from top-scored leads.
- Week 3-6:
  - Contact rate >= 40%
  - Meeting rate >= 45% (from contacted)
  - Proposal rate >= 40% (from meetings)
- Week 7-12:
  - Close rate >= 25% (from proposals)
  - Monthly recognized revenue >= 20M KRW

## 6) Risk Controls
- Data risk:
  - Use `regression_check.py` before business reporting.
  - Treat quality != FULL as soft incident.
- Execution risk:
  - Keep `autopilot.py --continue-on-error` scheduled weekly.
  - Use `result/next_actions_latest.md` as mandatory weekly checklist.
- Concentration risk:
  - Limit account concentration by region and ordering organization.

## 7) Stabilization Backlog (Engineering)
- P0 (now)
  - Keep latest CSV/JSON contracts stable.
  - Webhook alerts on config/dependency/data failures.
  - Auto-generate business outputs from latest run.
- P1 (next)
  - Improve category normalization for `기타` reduction.
  - Add contact-completeness and region-completeness data quality KPIs.
- P2 (later)
  - CRM integration (status tracking per lead row).
  - Add conversion learning loop for pricing and close probability.

## 8) Weekly Operating Checklist
- Run:
  - `py autopilot.py --continue-on-error`
- Review:
  - `result/revenue_plan_latest.json`
  - `result/business_brainstorm_latest.md`
  - `result/sales_queue_latest.csv` top 20 rows
- Execute:
  - Call and email top 20 leads
  - Record outcomes
  - Adjust pricing/package assumptions weekly
